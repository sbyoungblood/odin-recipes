Ham & Bean Soup

  • Home

Ingredients

  • 1 can of great northern beans
  • 1 cup shredded ham
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Green onions (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Mince garlic and dice the onion. Chop green onions.
  2. Heat oil in a 5 quart cooking pot.
  3. Saute onion and garlic in the pot until onion is transluscent.
  4. Add ham to pot.
  5. Saute for 2 more minutes.
  6. Add chicken broth and spices. Stir well.
  7. Let simmer on low heat, covered, for 45 minutes.
  8. Drain and rinse beans.
  9. After 45 minutes is up, add beans and let simmer for another 10 minutes on low heat, covered.
  10. Serve, garnish with green onions, and enjoy.
